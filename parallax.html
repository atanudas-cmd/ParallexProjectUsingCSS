About area



Over the years ADA Software has developed its expertise in the re-engineering and modernization of legacy application software and has delivered projects involving large and complex Line of Business Applications for industries as diverse as Insurance, Media Distribution and Retail etc. ADA Software specializes in legacy software migration and modernization, utilizing OMG's Architecture Driven Modernization (ADM) and Model Driven Architecture's (MDA) approaches. This company was established in 1999 by a group of IT Professionals, with experience of running data centers since early 80s using IBM & ICL Mainframes as well as Unix boxes. Based in Kolkata-INDIA, ADA Software was incorporated to provide software development services to clients in India, Europe and North America.
